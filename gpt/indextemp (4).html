What is Panchakarma?

The Five Sacred Actions

Panchakarma, meaning "five actions," is the cornerstone of Ayurvedic detoxification. These five therapeutic procedures work synergistically to eliminate toxins, balance doshas, and restore your natural state of health and vitality.

Vamana: Therapeutic emesis for Kapha imbalances

Virechana: Purification therapy for Pitta disorders

Basti: Medicated enemas for Vata restoration

Nasya: Nasal administration for head and neck region

Raktamokshana: Blood purification therapy
